My Journalism Work

I am inspired by good journalism. I love telling stories through people. I also like adding my own flavor into it! It brings me great joy knowing that I am contributing to the world, by giving people a platform to tell their stories. I have learned that there are three sides to every story: Person A's side, Person B's side, and the truth. It is not my job to decide who is right nor wrong, but to provide the world with the facts, and allow them to decide. I love this artform known as journalism, and I hope to continue to put my spin on it all I will continue to do my best in this.

In the words of Rihanna, "work,work,work,work,work".
